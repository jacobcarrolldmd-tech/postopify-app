Post-Op Instructions

📋 Purpose

This app is a medication tracking tool only. It helps you log when you take your prescribed medications after your procedure.

⚡ Not Medical Advice

Always follow your surgeon's instructions and your prescription label. Never exceed your prescribed dosage.

🔒 Privacy

All information stays on your device only. Nothing is shared with your provider or any third party.

⚠️ Emergency: Call 911 immediately for severe allergic reactions or life-threatening symptoms.

Important Reminders
🚫Do NOT take ibuprofen on an empty stomach — always eat first.
🚫Do NOT combine pain medication with alcohol, sleep aids, or other sedatives.
🚗Do NOT drive while taking opioid/narcotic pain medication.
💊Take your antibiotic at the same time each day and FINISH the full course.
📞Call our office if pain is uncontrolled, you have a fever >101°F, or signs of infection.
🚨Emergency: Call 911 or go to the nearest ER for severe allergic reactions.
